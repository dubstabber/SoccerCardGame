Soccer Card Game

How to Play Soccer Card Game

Game Overview

Soccer Card Game is a turn-based strategic card game where you compete against the AI to score the most goals.

The game is played over 2 rounds, and the player with the highest score at the end wins.

Card Types

  • Attack Cards: Used to score goals (marked as "attack" on the card)
  • Goal Cards: Used to defend against attacks (marked as "goal" on the card)

Each card comes in 5 different colors: Black, Blue, Brown, Red, and Golden.

Game Flow

  1. You and the AI take turns playing cards - either attacking or defending.
  2. When it's your turn to attack, select an attack card from your hand (bottom of screen).
  3. When it's your turn to defend, select a goal card that matches the color of the AI's attack.
  4. Cards played are shown in the center of the board.

Scoring Points

  • To score a goal: Play an attack card when the AI can't defend with a matching color goal card.
  • To prevent the AI from scoring: Play a goal card with the same color as the AI's attack card.

The score is displayed at the top of the game board, with your score on the left.

Tip: Pay attention to which cards the AI has already played to predict if they can defend against certain colors!
