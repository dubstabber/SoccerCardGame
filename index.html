Soccer Card Game

You play with the cards at the bottom of the screen.

There are two types of them: attacking card and defending card.

Each type has 5 different colors: black, blue, brown, red and yellow.

In order to score a point you have to click on an attacking card with the desired color.

If AI has the defending card in its deck with the same color the player used to attack then no point is scored.

Selected cards are being shown on the center.

The turns are alternating so if you were an attacker then next time you probably are going to prepare for defending.

You have to use an appropriate defending card in order to prevent AI from scoring.

Your points are on the left of the Score bar.
